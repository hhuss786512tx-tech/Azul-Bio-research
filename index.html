OUR SITES

Fully Equipped Facilities

Our clinical sites feature state-of-the-art diagnostic and storage systems to maintain protocol integrity.

CLIA-Certified Laboratories: High-integrity sample collection and diagnostic processing.
IATA-Certified Staff: Trained for safe handling and domestic/international bio-shipping.
Cold Chain Management: Refrigerators (2 - 8°C), Freezers (-70°C), and local dry ice access.
Temperature Excursion Alerts: 24-hour recordable devices with active warning alerts.
PBMC Processing: On-site ambient and refrigerated centrifuges for complex cell isolation.
Infusion Center: Fully equipped and monitored by trained clinical nurses.

IP Storage Room

Dedicated locked/controlled investigational product access storage to secure drug supply chains.

Experienced Oversight

GCP-trained physicians and staff operating with central IRB acceptance for rapid study startup.
